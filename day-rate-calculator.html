Hourly vs. day rate — which should you quote?

Day rates are common for consulting and on-site work where a client books your full focus for the day, regardless of exact hours logged. They simplify invoicing but carry the same scope-creep risk as any fixed structure if "a day" quietly stretches longer. This converter assumes a constant hours-per-day input — for a full risk-adjusted profitability view of a real project, run it through the main rate calculator.
